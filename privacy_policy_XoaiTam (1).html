Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 14, 2025

English

UGMobile respects and protects the privacy of all users. This app only requires basic permissions to provide its features:

  • Internet access: to connect to online services.
  • Camera and Microphone (if enabled): only used for video calls inside the app, not stored or shared.

UGMobile does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

If you have questions, please contact us at: XoaiTam
